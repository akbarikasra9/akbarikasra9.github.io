When it comes to the similarities and differences between presidents among time is that some US presidents didn’t use the word “American” too frequent. It became more frequent later because it was used as a key identity during times ,such as World War 2 and the Cold War. After 1949, the terms “American”, and “people” appeared more throughout speeches by US Presidents John F. Kennedy and Ronald Reagan. The term “capitalist”, was less common but more concentrated in specific contexts such as the Cold War.

Additionally, the quanteda websites defines Wordfish as a Poisson scaling model that estimates one-dimension document positions that utilizes maximum likelihoods, and both the estimated position of words and the documents can be plotted.
